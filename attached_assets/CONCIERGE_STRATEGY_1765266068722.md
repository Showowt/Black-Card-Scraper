## OUTREACH STRATEGY

### Why Concierge is Different

Standard hospitality outreach: **IG → WhatsApp → Email**

Concierge outreach: **LinkedIn → Email → WhatsApp**

**Why:**
- They're more "professional" than restaurant owners
- They check email (managing wealthy clients)
- LinkedIn = credibility matters more
- WhatsApp feels more invasive for service providers

### The Concierge-Specific Hook

**Don't lead with AI. Lead with understanding.**

```
"You're probably the only person standing between 
10 demanding guests and chaos right now. 

Quick question - how are you tracking client preferences 
across visits? Or is it all in your head and WhatsApp history?"
```

**Psychology Angle:**
- They PRIDE themselves on being indispensable
- But secretly they're EXHAUSTED
- They want to scale but CAN'T clone themselves
- Identity: "The fixer who makes impossible things happen"
