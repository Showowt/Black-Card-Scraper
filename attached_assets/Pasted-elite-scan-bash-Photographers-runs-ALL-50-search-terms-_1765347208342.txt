```
## How Elite Scan Works
```
1. Takes your category (e.g., "photographer")
2. Gets ALL 50+ search terms for that category
3. Runs EACH term as a separate Google Places search
4. Deduplicates by place_id (no duplicates)
5. Saves ALL unique results to database
6. Shows you what it found
