### How It Decides What to Pitch

The system analyzes signals and picks the RIGHT offer:

**Restaurant Signals:**
| Signal | Detected Problem | Your Offer |
|--------|------------------|------------|
| No website | No online booking | AI booking bot via WhatsApp |
| Low review response | Reputation damage | Review monitoring + AI responses |
| Has WhatsApp but manual | Owner drowning | WhatsApp AI assistant |
| High reviews (200+) | Popular but chaotic | Full operational automation |

**Hotel Signals:**
| Signal | Detected Problem | Your Offer |
|--------|------------------|------------|
| OTA dependent | Paying 15-25% commission | Direct booking system + AI concierge |
| Slow response | Losing to faster competitors | 24/7 AI concierge |
| No upsells | Missing ancillary revenue | Automated pre-arrival upsell sequence |

**Concierge Signals:**
| Signal | Detected Problem | Your Offer |
|--------|------------------|------------|
| No client database | Preferences in head/WhatsApp | AI client preference system |
| Vendor chaos | Manual coordination hell | Vendor coordination automation |
| 24/7 expectation | Burning out | AI first-response system |

**+ Tour Operator, Boat Charter, Villa Rental** - all have specific offer matrices.

### Instagram Discovery

The system tries multiple sources:

1. **Existing data** - From Google Places API or website scrape (HIGH confidence)
2. **Website scraping** - Looks for IG links on their site (HIGH confidence)
3. **Generated handles** - Creates likely handles like `lavitrola`, `lavitrola_cartagena` (LOW confidence - verify before sending)
